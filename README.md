# Too Hectic

A comic series by Zach Trover & Shawn DePasquale.

Rockville, 1995. Two best friends. A town full of monsters, aliens, robots, 
corporate conspiracies, and deeply questionable taco opinions. 

Too Hectic is an ongoing comic series blending supernatural chaos 
with 90s nostalgia, ska music, and the kind of friendship that 
survives resurrecting dead musicians with a flesh-bound Book of the Dead.
